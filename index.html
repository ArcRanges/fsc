Welcome to FSC

Our Little Story

I come from the great archipelago of the Philippines and it has always been my passion to cook for people. Back home, I had a restaurant and I really loved serving people authentic and traditional Filipino foods. Since the day that I've stepped on the great land of Canada, it has been my dream to start a restaurant here: not just to serve people, but to make known of the unique delicacies of my great country.

Carinderia is a Tagalog word which means "affordable restaurant".

"Cooking is like painting or writing a song. Just as there are only so many notes or colors, there are only so many flavours - it's how you combine them that sets you apart."

Wolfgang Puck
